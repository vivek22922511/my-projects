About Shanti Institute

Shanti Institute of Engineering College is committed to excellence in education, innovation, and holistic student development. Established in 2008 by the Shanti Educational Society, our campus fosters a strong academic and practical foundation for aspiring engineers and business professionals.

We are affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), approved by AICTE, and dedicated to delivering quality technical education through highly experienced faculty, state-of-the-art labs, and industry interaction.
